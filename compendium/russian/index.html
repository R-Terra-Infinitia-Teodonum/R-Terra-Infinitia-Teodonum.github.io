Russian

russianforeveryone is a great resource for studying Russian, together with the many YouTube videos on the language.

If I were to study Russian from the beginning now, I would get some basic Grammar down with russianforeveryone, watch videos and learn some vocabulary on YouTube, and then start playing with reading and thinking in Russian quick.

The pronunciation isn’t that hard. The language you will learn easily - if this is scary to you, you’ll be fine, it’s not that much compared to Japanese or Chinese, though that too won’t be hard with the right methods...

Typing Speed

Typing speed is for everyone. If you spend one day learning to use a laptop keyboard properly you will:

- Gain confidence in all typing (and your whole life);

- Gain the time spent many times over just for your ability to now type at any pace;

- Thank yourself a lot later, over years;

- Give you a skill you can use and empower you to use your laptop creatively;

- Make writing things by hand rather than copying them easy forever, allowing you to write templates rather than copying them, at times, as what can be a great learning experience where you re-do the basics.

All for just one day and another few getting your speed up in a new typing style that gives you TRUE COMOFRT and EFFORTLESS writing.

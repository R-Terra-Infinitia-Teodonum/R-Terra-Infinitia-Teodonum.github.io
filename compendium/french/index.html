French

French can be learnt well-enough with the Michel Thomas tapes, with Duolingo aaiding your pronounciation.

Then, you can practice speaking French to yourself and viewing the world in French colors - a new perspective.

This happens to be what anyone that becomes good at a language does, getting good at having a *mode* of yourself in x language.

Pronunciation also matters! For French, you may find a French channel you like to act as your role *model* after a week or two on Duolingo

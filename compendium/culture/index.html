Culture

We begin our discussion of human culture by looking at the rough cultural distinctions of West and East.

The West tends to be about individual empowerment whilst the East is about taking responsability for the happienss of others, often because of a feeling of debt to others, which is confused for love.

This leads to bitterness often, and is generally the root of the ”pajeet” archetype.

In the west, our self-centeredness is more often about greed and personal gain at the expense of others.
